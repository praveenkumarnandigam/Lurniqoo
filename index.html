Introduction

Lurniqoo is a student-focused learning platform designed to simplify access to syllabi, study materials, and structured academic resources. It helps university students quickly find what they need without confusion or delay.

By organizing content by department, year, semester, and subject, Lurniqoo brings clarity to the academic journey and reduces the stress of searching across scattered platforms.
